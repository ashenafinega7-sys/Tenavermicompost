Usage Instructions

Always mix well with soil and water after application.

Seedlings / Vegetables

Apply 250–500 g per plant around the root zone. Incorporate lightly into the topsoil.

Cereals & Field Crops

Broadcast 100–200 kg per hectare and incorporate before planting or at early growth stages.

Flowers & Gardens

Apply 1–2 handfuls per plant (approx. 80–150 g) and mulch over.

Fruit Trees

Apply 2–5 kg evenly around the drip line; gently mix into soil.
